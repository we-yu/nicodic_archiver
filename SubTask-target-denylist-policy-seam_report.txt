Summary
- Added a small shared denylist policy seam for known high-volume articles.
- Target registration now rejects denylisted inputs at the common
  registration boundary and surfaces the result to web, feeder, and import
  intake paths.

  before resolution or registration and counted separately.
- Scrape-side denylist protection in orchestrator remains in place as a
  safety net.

Known limitations
- Denylist matching is keyed primarily by numeric NicoNicoPedia article id.
- Supplementary exact URL matching is limited to the known denylisted
  canonical article URLs and id URLs in the policy seam.
- Raw denylisted feeder pre-skip is limited to /id/<digits> inputs that are
  directly observable before the existing resolution path.
- No fuzzy title matching, DB-backed denylist, schema flags, or extra network
  requests were added solely for denylist checks.
